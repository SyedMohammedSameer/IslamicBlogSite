Lining changes thickness

During the menstrual cycle the thickness of the womb lining (Endometrium) increases several folds (1-16mm). This was however recently, however this was portrayed in the Quran 1400 years before it was discovered.

"What to know about endometrial thickness

The endometrium is the lining of the uterus. The body prepares the endometrium to host an embryo during the menstrual cycle. Depending on what stage of the cycle a person is in, endometrial thickness varies from 1–18 millimeters (mm)."

Medical News Today, What to know about endometrial thickness, 2025

The thickness of the womb lining (Endometrium) increases between 1-16mm; this was known recently, however this was portrayed in the Quran 1400 before it was descovered.

Quran 13:8

Allah knows what every female bears, and every decrease and increase to the wombs. With Him, everything is by measure.

٨ اللَّهُ يَعْلَمُ مَا تَحْمِلُ كُلُّ أُنْثَىٰ وَمَا تَغِيضُ الْأَرْحَامُ وَمَا تَزْدَادُ ۖ وَكُلُّ شَيْءٍ عِنْدَهُ بِمِقْدَارٍ

Their is decreases and increases in the womb, today we know that the thickness  of the lining changes.
