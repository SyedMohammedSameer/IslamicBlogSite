Isotropy

Cosmology - Advanced

Continue

No preferred directions in space.

In the Quran their is no preferred direction in outer-space. Skeptics claim that whoever wrote the Quran made a mistake; the preferred direction is the center of the universe. Today cosmologists confirm that the universe has no center and there is no preferred directions in outer-space.

"Definition of Isotropy

No preferred directions in space. Definition of Isotropy Qualitatively, isotropy refers to the absence of preferred directions in space."

'Primordial Cosmology', Montani Battisti Benini Imponente, World Scientific (2011) page 97.

There are no preferred directions in space. This was known recently, however this was portrayed in the Quran 1400 years ago before it was discovered:

Quran 24:35

Allah is the Light of the heavens and the earth. The allegory of His light is that of a pillar on which is a lamp. The lamp is within a glass. The glass is like a brilliant planet, fueled by a blessed tree, an olive tree, neither eastern nor western. Its oil would almost illuminate, even if no fire has touched it. Light upon Light. Allah guides to His light whomever He wills. Allah thus cites the parables for the people. Allah is cognizant of everything.

٣٥ اللَّهُ نُورُ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضِ ۚ مَثَلُ نُورِهِ كَمِشْكَاةٍ فِيهَا مِصْبَاحٌ ۖ الْمِصْبَاحُ فِي زُجَاجَةٍ ۖ الزُّجَاجَةُ كَأَنَّهَا كَوْكَبٌ دُرِّيٌّ يُوقَدُ مِنْ شَجَرَةٍ مُبَارَكَةٍ زَيْتُونَةٍ لَا شَرْقِيَّةٍ وَلَا غَرْبِيَّةٍ يَكَادُ زَيْتُهَا يُضِيءُ وَلَوْ لَمْ تَمْسَسْهُ نَارٌ ۚ نُورٌ عَلَىٰ نُورٍ ۗ يَهْدِي اللَّهُ لِنُورِهِ مَنْ يَشَاءُ ۚ وَيَضْرِبُ اللَّهُ الْأَمْثَالَ لِلنَّاسِ ۗ وَاللَّهُ بِكُلِّ شَيْءٍ عَلِيمٌ

"Neither eastern nor western" means no preferred directions in space.

How could an illiterate man who lived 1400 years ago have known about isotropy?
