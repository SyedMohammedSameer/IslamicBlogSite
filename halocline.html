Halocline

Chemistry - Extreme

Continue

Water Stratification.

In the Quran there is a barrier between two seas with different salinities. Skeptics claim that whoever wrote the Quran made a mistake; they will mix to a new average salinity. Today scientists confirm that halocline separates two bodies of water with different salinities.

The difference in density, temperature, salinity and CO2 concentrations in seas cause water to separate into layers. This phenomenon is called water stratification. 

In the Arctic oceanographers discovered a layer 150m thick that acts as a barrier between the freshwater above it and the salty water below it. This barrier is called halocline.

"Halocline

In oceanography, a halocline is a subtype of chemocline, caused by a strong vertical salinity gradient within a body of water. Because salinity (in concert with temperature) affects the density of seawater, it can play a role in its vertical stratification."

Wikipedia, Halocline, 2018

A halocline separates water layers with different salinities. This was only known recently, however this was portrayed in the Quran 1400 years before it was discovered.

Quran 25:53

And it is He who merged the two seas; this one fresh and pure, and that one salty and bitter; and He placed between them a barrier and a boundary.

٥٣ وَهُوَ الَّذِي مَرَجَ الْبَحْرَيْنِ هَٰذَا عَذْبٌ فُرَاتٌ وَهَٰذَا مِلْحٌ أُجَاجٌ وَجَعَلَ بَيْنَهُمَا بَرْزَخًا وَحِجْرًا مَحْجُورًا

The Quran correctly described sea water separation caused by difference in salinity. Today we know what it is, this is a halocline.

How could an illiterate man who lived 1400 years ago have known about halocline?
